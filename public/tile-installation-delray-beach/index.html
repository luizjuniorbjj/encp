What Delray Beach Homeowners Should Know About Tile Installation

The average cost for tile installation in Delray Beach typically ranges from $10 to $20 per square foot, depending on the materials chosen and the complexity of the job. Most installations can take anywhere from one to three days, depending on the size and scope of the project. With the high humidity, selecting the right materials is crucial—porcelain tiles are often recommended over ceramic due to their water-resistant properties, making them ideal for bathrooms and kitchens.

A common mistake homeowners make is underestimating the importance of proper waterproofing in wet areas. Without it, even the best tiles can suffer from damage and mold, leading to expensive repairs down the line. Investing in quality installation upfront will save you both time and money in the long run.
